
NAPALM Ex3
-----------

Using NAPALM and the one of the IOS devices perform the following config 
operations.

a. Stage a change to add a /32 static route (merge operation)
   Use something in 1.1.X.X.
b. Perform a compare_config operation to see your staged change.
c. Discard your change.
d. Verify compare_config shows no changes pending.
e. Re-stage your change adding a /32 static route (merge operation).
f. Commit your change.
